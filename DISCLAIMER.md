The tool performs simple HTTP response header checks against websites explicitly provided by the user. It does not exploit vulnerabilities, bypass access controls, brute-force endpoints, or perform intrusive scanning.

Cross-origin redirects are blocked by default. Do not enable them unless every possible destination is within your authorized scope.

Use the tool only on systems you own, administer, or are authorized to assess. The output is informational and should not be treated as a complete security assessment.
